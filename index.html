Mark Cavendish-Cav-The Manx Missile

Fast Sprinter, Fast Thinker, Fast Talker

Mark Cavendish MBE has always been an avid cyclist, riding a BMX from a young age led to him racing at the National Sports Centre in Douglas. Having experienced success on the track Mark joined T-Mobile in 2006 as a stagiaire. During the 2008 season, Mark made his first big impact on the Grand Tours, winning a fantastic two stages in the Giro d’Italia followed by four victories at the Tour de France, becoming the most successful British cyclist overnight at the age of 22.

Holding joint honours with the legendary Eddy Mercx for the most stage wins at the Tour De France (34), 'Cav' is one of the most decorated cyclists of all time. Below is a list of the highlights

  • Tour De France
    • Points classification - 2011/21
    • Stage wins (34) - 2008/ 09/ 10/ 11/ 12/ 13/ 15/ 16/ 21
  • Giro D'Italia
    • Points classification - 2013
    • Stage wins (15) - 2008/ 09/ 11/ 12/ 13
  • Vuelta a Espana
    • Points classification - 2010
    • Stage wins (3) - 2010
  • One Day Races and Classics
    • Milan - San-Remo - 2009
    • Grote Scheldeprijs - 2007/ 08/ 11
    • Kuurne–Brussels–Kuurne - 2012/ 15
    • World Championships - 2011
    • National Championships - 2013
